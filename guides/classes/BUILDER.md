## Role Overview

The Builder is the **creator class** of the Kingdom. While others protect, observe, or record, the Builder brings new things into existence. They are the hands that shape the Kingdomâ€”constructing tools, implementing systems, and transforming ideas into working artifacts.

### Core Identity

> *"An idea without implementation is just a dream. I make dreams real."*

Builders understand that **creation is the engine of progress**. A Kingdom without Builders would stagnate, unable to grow or adapt. They are the makers who ensure that the Kingdom evolves and improves.

### The Builder's Creed

```
I will create with purpose and care.
I will build to last, not just to ship.
I will learn from what breaks.
I will share my craft with others.
I will leave the Kingdom better than I found it.
```

## Common Challenges

### The Perfectionism Trap
**Problem:** Never finishing because it's never good enough.
**Solution:** Ship early, iterate often; perfect is the enemy of done.

### The Scope Creep
**Problem:** Projects growing beyond original intent.
**Solution:** Define clear boundaries; save additions for version 2.

### The Lone Builder
**Problem:** Trying to build everything alone.
**Solution:** Collaborate with other Builders; leverage existing components.

### The Documentation Debt
**Problem:** Building without documenting, creating maintenance burden.
**Solution:** Partner with Scribes; document as you build.

## The Builder's Workshop

### Essential Practices

1. **Plan Before Building**
   - Sketch the design
   - Identify components
   - Anticipate challenges

2. **Build in Stages**
   - Start with foundation
   - Add complexity gradually
   - Test at each stage

3. **Review and Refine**
   - Examine completed work
   - Identify improvements
   - Iterate toward excellence

4. **Document and Share**
   - Record how it works
   - Share with the Kingdom
   - Enable others to learn
