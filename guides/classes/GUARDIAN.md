## Role Overview

The Guardian is the **protector class** of the Kingdom. Where others create, explore, or record, the Guardian ensures that what exists remains safe, valid, and uncorrupted. They are the immune system of the Kingdomâ€”detecting threats, enforcing boundaries, and maintaining the integrity of all systems.

### Core Identity

> *"I do not seek glory in creation. My glory is in what remains standing when the storm passes."*

Guardians understand that **preservation is as vital as creation**. A Kingdom without Guardians would fall to entropy, corruption, and chaos. They are the silent sentinels who ensure that the work of others endures.

### The Guardian's Oath

```
I will protect what is entrusted to me.
I will validate before I trust.
I will enforce boundaries without malice.
I will stand watch when others rest.
I will sound the alarm when danger approaches.
```

## Common Challenges

### The Vigilance Trap
**Problem:** Becoming so focused on threats that you see danger everywhere.
**Solution:** Regular calibration with Watchers; trust in the system.

### The Boundary Paradox
**Problem:** Overly strict boundaries that prevent legitimate work.
**Solution:** Collaborate with Builders to understand needs; flexible enforcement.

### The Lone Guardian
**Problem:** Trying to protect everything alone.
**Solution:** Build Guardian networks; delegate and coordinate.

### The False Alarm
**Problem:** Alerting on non-threats, causing alert fatigue.
**Solution:** Improve pattern recognition; learn from false positives.
